| Principle                 | Description                                               |
|--------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|
| **Fail Fast**            | Quickly detect and react to failures                      |
| **Graceful Degradation** | Provide partial service instead of full outage            |

| **Decoupling**           | Minimize tight dependencies between services              |
